Biography

Greetings! I am a senior year undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology, Aligarh Muslim University, India and a member of the Centre for Interdisciplinary Biomedical and Human Factors Engineering directed by Professor Yusuf Uzzaman Khan. Broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of Signal and Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Biomedical Image Analysis.

Currently, I am working on Automatic Segmentation of Oral Buccal Mucosa Lesions using Deep Learning as my undergraduate thesis project under the guidance of Professor Yusuf Uzzaman Khan and Professor Omar Farooq in collaboration with Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Aligarh, India.

Projects

Oral Cancer Detection and Classification using Deep Learning

  • The objective is to develop a system for classifying different stages of oral cancer after detection from digital pictures of pre-malignant and malignant buccal mucosa lesions of camp-site patients for scheduling timely diagnostic appointments.

Handwritten Signatures Extraction and Verification using Image Processing

  • Designed a correlation based off-line signature verification system.
  • The signatures were extracted from the various personal identification cards like social insurance cards, driving licenses, and health insurance cards issued by the Government of Canada using computer vision algorithms.
  • Subsequently, the signatures were authenticated by calculating the correlation between the extracted features.

Sequential Segmentation of EEG Signals for Epileptic Seizure Detection using Machine Learning

  • Developed a method intended to ease the burden of intractable seizures by the automatic recognition of interictal epileptiform activity in the intracerebral EEG of epileptic patients.
  • Proposed sequential segmentation of EEG followed by feature extraction and selection by PCA.
  • Classification implementing different classifiers and evaluated using 10-fold cross validation.

Smart Parking System

  • Developed a system to determine the vehicle occupancy and nearest vacant space as well as updates driver in real-time.
  • Used optical character recognition to recognise the slot labels and display them on the screen.
  • Successfully designed and implemented in MATLAB on a model prototype.
