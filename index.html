Biography

Greetings! I am a senior year Electrical Engineering undergraduate from Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology, Aligarh Muslim University, India and is a member of the Centre for Interdisciplinary Biomedical and Human Factors Engineering directed by Professor Yusuf Uzzaman Khan. Currently, I am working as a Research Intern under Prof. Darren Abramson, Dalhousie University, Canada remotely. My contribution has been in working with datasets for Compositional Tasks in Natural Language Understanding, preparing them for pretraining of State-Of-The-Art Language Models. I have also contributed in getting the results on Benchmark Datasets for Common-Sense-Reasoning Tasks using Zero-Shot Measurements.

I am interested in research that lies at the intersection of Machine Learning and Cognitive Sciences. I have hands-on experience with a variety of different NLP projects. I have a passion for building ideas and products for social good with the use of AI/ML. Recently, me and my team won the Popular Choice Award at Microsoft's Azure AI Hackathon 2021.

My curosity about the philosiphical and psychological ideas explaining the phenomenons not explained by science, makes me fiddle around the ways that can explain those ideas on empirical data. Apart from it, I love Music, Theatre, and Books.
