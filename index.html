Biography

Greetings! I am a senior year undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology, Aligarh Muslim University, India and am a member of the Centre for Interdisciplinary Biomedical and Human Factors Engineering directed by Professor Yusuf Uzzaman Khan. Broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of Signal and Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Biomedical Image Analysis.

Currently, I am working on Oral Cancer Detection and Classification using Deep Learning as my undergraduate thesis project under the guidance of Professor Yusuf Uzzaman Khan and Professor Omar Farooq in collaboration with Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Aligarh, India.
